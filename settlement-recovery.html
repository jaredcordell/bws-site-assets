Your business may already be owed money.

Companies settle class-action and antitrust claims constantly, for overcharges on payment processing, freight, software, and more. Most businesses never file. We find what you're owed and recover it for you, on a no-recovery, no-fee basis.

Why this money goes unclaimed

Most businesses don't know they're eligible. The settlements are real, the claims just never get filed.

Notices get missed

Settlement notices arrive as legal junk mail and get tossed. The deadline passes, the money goes back.

Filing is tedious

Claims require documentation and follow-up most owners never have time for.

Nobody's watching

New settlements open constantly. Without someone tracking them for you, you simply never hear about it.

Are you eligible?

Three things that make businesses eligible. If any of these apply, it's worth a free review.

Often eligible

  • ✓You accept credit cards (processing overcharge settlements)
  • ✓You ship freight or use carriers (surcharge settlements)
  • ✓You buy software, tech, or financial products at scale
  • ✓You've been in business for several years
  • ✓You operate in food service, healthcare, or distribution

Typically not eligible

  • ×Brand-new businesses with little transaction history
  • ×No qualifying purchases in covered categories
  • ×No records available for the relevant period

Common questions

Claims recovery questions, answered.

Is this legitimate?

Yes. These are real, court-approved class-action and antitrust settlements. The money is set aside specifically for affected businesses, most simply never claim it.

What does it cost?

Nothing upfront. We work on a contingency basis: our fee comes only from funds we actually recover for you. No recovery, no fee.

How much work is involved on my end?

Very little. We tell you which records we need (usually documents you already keep), and we handle the filing and follow-up.

What if I already throw out those notices?

That's exactly the problem we solve. We monitor open and upcoming settlements for you so nothing slips by again.
