What we cover

Four core coverages that protect what you've built. Bundle them or start with what you need most.

Commercial Property

Protects your building, equipment, inventory, and physical assets from fire, theft, storms, and other covered losses, so a bad day doesn't become a closed business.

Commercial Auto

Coverage for the vehicles your business owns, leases, or uses, liability, collision, and physical damage, keeping your drivers and your operation on the road.

General Liability

Protection against third-party claims of bodily injury, property damage, and the legal costs that come with them, the foundation of nearly every business insurance program.

Workers' Compensation

Required in most states, it covers medical bills and lost wages if an employee is hurt on the job, protecting both your team and your business from what follows.

Common questions

Commercial insurance questions, answered.

Who actually provides the insurance?

Coverage is placed through Creekside Insurance Advisors, Inc., a licensed commercial insurance agency. We connect you with their team, who work directly with you to prepare quotes and service your policy.

What does it cost to get a quote?

Nothing. Requesting quotes is completely free and there's no obligation to buy. You only move forward if the coverage and pricing make sense for your business.

Which coverages can I get?

Right now we're focused on the four coverages most businesses need: commercial property, commercial auto, general liability, and workers' compensation. You can request one or all of them.

Do I need to switch from my current agent to get a quote?

No. Many business owners simply want a second look at their current coverage and pricing. We're happy to review it, you decide what to do from there.

Do I have to answer every question to get started?

No. Give us what you have and submit your request. If any additional details would help build your quotes, Creekside will follow up with you directly, nothing needs to hold up your request.

How long does it take?

It starts the moment you submit your request. Timing depends on the coverages and information involved, but keeping in touch with your advisor is the fastest way to get your quotes back quickly.
