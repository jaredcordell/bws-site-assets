Nick Yocca

Nick Yocca is the Co-founder of Big Wolf Solutions, a business consulting and financial optimization firm focused on helping companies recover overlooked revenue, reduce unnecessary expenses, and improve operational profitability.

Nick attended Stetson University, where he earned a Bachelor’s degree in Accounting and Information Systems. He also spent more than 15 years working as a high-level software engineer, giving him a strong technical and analytical foundation that continues to influence his strategic approach to business operations, financial systems, automation, and problem-solving.

With more than two decades of experience working with business owners across multiple industries, Nick has built a reputation for identifying opportunities that many companies and advisors miss. Over the years, Nick has developed specialized expertise in merchant services, including high-risk processing, Level 3 optimization, and eCommerce payment systems. His current work also centers around Research & Development tax credit recovery, class action settlement discovery, and strategic business consulting designed to strengthen the bottom line.

In addition to his business ventures, Nick and his wife, Ali, are the co-founders and directors of Second Chance Rescue & Sanctuary, an animal rescue organization dedicated to helping neglected, abandoned, and at-risk animals find safe homes and second chances. A portion of proceeds from Big Wolf Solutions helps support ongoing rescue operations, medical care, and rehabilitation for animals in our care.

Nick works closely with a network of industry professionals, third-party CPAs, tax attorneys, and strategic partners to help ensure every solution delivered through Big Wolf Solutions is accurate, compliant, and tailored to the client’s business model and growth objectives.

Known for his direct, relationship-driven approach, Nick focuses on simplifying complex financial opportunities into practical, actionable strategies that business owners can clearly understand and implement. His goal is to help companies retain more of what they earn while creating long-term operational and financial advantages.

Through Big Wolf Solutions, Nick continues to build strategic partnerships with business consultants, agencies, financial professionals, and industry leaders nationwide to deliver high-value solutions that create measurable impact for clients.

Ali Yocca

Ali Yocca is a business strategist, entrepreneur, and operations leader with a passion for building companies that combine innovation, purpose, and long-term growth. As Co-Founder of Big Wolf Solutions, Ali works closely with business owners to identify opportunities that strengthen operations, improve profitability, and uncover overlooked financial advantages that help businesses scale more effectively.

A graduate of Miami University with a Bachelor of Science in Marketing, Ali brings a strong foundation in business development, branding, and customer engagement. Her professional background includes more than a decade in retail management, where she developed extensive experience in leadership, operations, team development, customer experience, and strategic problem solving.

Since 2010, Ali has owned and operated multiple businesses, gaining firsthand experience in what it takes to transform an idea into a fully functioning and sustainable company. Her entrepreneurial background includes building and managing a large community event production and marketing company, giving her practical insight into business growth, operational structure, branding, and customer engagement.

With expertise spanning business development, marketing compliance, brand standards, operational strategy, and client communication, Ali is known for blending creativity with structure to help organizations simplify complex processes and create scalable systems for growth. She plays an active role in guiding digital presence, brand consistency, and strategic communication initiatives, helping businesses present themselves with professionalism, clarity, and trust.

In addition to her business leadership, Ali is also Co-Founder of Second Chance Animal Rescue and Sanctuary, a nonprofit 501(c)(3) organization dedicated to rescuing and caring for animals in need. Through the work of Big Wolf Solutions, Ali and her team are committed to supporting causes that create meaningful community impact, with a portion of company proceeds helping support the mission and ongoing operations of Second Chance Animal Rescue and Sanctuary.

Outside of business, Ali enjoys spending time with family, caring for sanctuary animals, and continuing to learn new ways to make a positive difference in the lives of others.
