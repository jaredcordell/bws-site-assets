Common questions

Bookkeeping questions, answered.

Who actually does the bookkeeping?

Your books are handled by TruePath Solutions, a bookkeeping and operational-support firm serving small and mid-sized businesses nationwide. We connect you and get you set up.

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

No. Services are flexible and scaled to your needs with no long-term commitment, you continue because it's working for you.

My books are a mess. Can you still help?

Yes. Cleanup and catch-up bookkeeping is a core service. We'll get prior periods sorted and bring you back to a clean, current set of records.

Is this cheaper than hiring a bookkeeper?

For most small and mid-sized businesses, yes. You get professional bookkeeping scaled to what you actually need, without the salary, benefits, and overhead of a full-time hire.

What do you need from me to get started?

Just a conversation to start. On a free discovery call we'll look at your current setup and recommend the right level of support, then handle the heavy lifting from there.

Can you help with more than bookkeeping?

Yes. TruePath also offers fractional admin and marketing support, business coaching, and talent sourcing, so support can grow with your business.
